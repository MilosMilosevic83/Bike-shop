Specifications

The F24 takes its cues from the adult F-series bikes. For the developing roadie, the FLight 6061 aluminum frame and carbon fiber fork make this just as serious a bike as the other F bikes. Because this bike is intended for smaller riders, it is built around 24” wheels to give adequate standover clearance and responsive handling. We use short-reach bars and levers for small hands as well as top-mount brake levers to offer as much control as possible. You needn’t be big to get big performance.
